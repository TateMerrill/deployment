The PLL Hype Page!!

The PLL (Premier Lacrosse League) is the professional lacrosse league in Northern America. The league consists of the best pros from around the world making up 8 teams. The 8 teams are Archers, Atlas, Cannons, Chaos, Chrome, Redwoods, Waterdogs, and Whipsnakes. The PLL is in its third season the Whipsnakes are the only team to win a championship. We are currently in the semi finals for the 2021 season. The final four teams are Atlas, Chaos, Waterdogs, and Whipsnakes. Will the Whipsnakes three-peat or will one of the other teams take the throne.
